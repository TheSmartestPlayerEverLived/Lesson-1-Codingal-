Pizza

Pizza is a flatbread dish of Italian origin, specifically from Naples, made with a dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients. Its origins trace back to ancient flatbreads, but the modern version emerged in 18th-century Naples as a cheap food for the working class. It has since become a globally popular and versatile food, celebrated for its wide variety of toppings and cultural significance
